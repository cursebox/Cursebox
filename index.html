Michael Negapatan, professionally known as Cursebox, is a young music producer, composer, DJ from the Philippines. He found out his niche in music production when he was 15, Cursebox is best known for producing, Hip Hop, Trap, R&B, Tropical Pop, Dancehall and Future Bass. He is also known for producing original tracks, "Chambe" by Alex Gonzaga and "Shopping" by Ryan Bang. He has produced for several artists and groups such as Ex Battalion, Skusta Clee, Flow G, Gloc 9, Josh Cullen of SB 19, Kris Lawrence, Jay R, Donnalyn Bartolome, ALAMAT, G22, Karencitta, Jake Zyrus, Vice Ganda, Alex Bruce, Andrea Brillantes, Angeline Quinto, Kyla and many more. and also commercial works for companies such as ABS-CBN, SM Supermalls, Coca Cola, Philtranco, Insular Life, Security Bank, Inquirer and Resorts World. He has also provided freelance work, arrangements, and music production services for Universal Music Group Philippines, Sony Music Philippines, ABS-CBN Star Music, Viva Records, and Def Jam Philippines. Furthermore, Cursebox achieved recognition as the Battle Of The Beats Philippines Champion in 2015 and is currently affiliated with the production team Panty Droppaz League.
